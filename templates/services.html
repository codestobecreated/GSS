FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our premium ceramic coatings last 5-7 years with proper maintenance. We provide detailed care instructions and offer maintenance packages to ensure longevity.

Can I remove a vehicle wrap later?

Yes! Vehicle wraps are designed to be removable without damaging the original paint. We use high-quality vinyl that can be safely removed when you're ready for a change.

How long does a full wrap take?

A full vehicle wrap typically takes 3-5 days depending on the complexity of the design and the vehicle size. We ensure every detail is perfect before delivery.

Do you offer warranties?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive warranties on all our services. Ceramic coatings come with a 5-year warranty, wraps with 3 years, and all workmanship is guaranteed.
